About

The website "St.Lawrence Strolls" aims to help you discover the best spots on the south shore of the St. Lawrence River, so you can fully enjoy everything it has to offer. From Québec City to Rimouski, our mission is to guide you in exploring new places—sometimes little-known, sometimes already cherished by the community. We are always open to your suggestions, which is why we have provided a form on our website for you to share your ideas. We hope the destinations we feature will spark your curiosity and inspire your adventures. The creator of "St.Lawrence Strolls", Charles-Antoine Fournier, native from Lévis, located in the beautiful Chaudière-Appalaches region.
